Storage Permission Usage

This application requests access to the device's storage permissions (including reading and writing external storage), mainly for saving and reading cached data, thereby improving application performance and user experience.

For example, the application will cache images, downloaded files or temporary data to the device storage to avoid repeated downloads, save traffic and loading time.

We will not collect or upload any of your personal information to the server through storage permissions. All cached data is stored only on your device.

You can manage application permissions in the device settings, or clean up cached data to free up storage space.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and data security and strictly complying with relevant laws and regulations.

Camera Permission Usage

This application requests access to the device camera permission, mainly for scanning code and taking photos.

Scanning the code with the camera can quickly identify QR codes or barcodes, making it convenient for you to use the related services provided by the application.

The photo taking function is used for users to actively upload or save pictures to improve the application interaction experience.

We will not record, store or share any videos or photos without your consent. The camera is only turned on when you explicitly authorize it, and all data is saved on your device.

You can manage app camera permissions in your device settings at any time to protect your privacy.

Push Notification Permission Usage

This application requests notification permission to send you important service-related alerts, such as order updates, abnormal transaction reminders, and system notifications.

To deliver push notifications, the app uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) and, on iOS, Apple Push Notification service (APNs). We may collect and store device push tokens on our servers and associate them with your account so notifications can be delivered to the correct device.

Push tokens are used only for notification delivery and related service functions. We do not use them for advertising or unrelated marketing without your consent.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device system settings. Disabling notifications may prevent you from receiving timely service alerts.
